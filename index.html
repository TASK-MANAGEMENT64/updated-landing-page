About Us

What is Task Management?

Task Management is a small business that focuses in creating software solutions for school and university students and leaders and members of a teleworking team (remote work). Our goal is to help them achieve adequate organization of their tasks and projects and, especially, meet their goals. Likewise, we follow a mutual learning model with our clients, in order to progressively improve our quality of care and the tools we offer them. In order to do this, we have designed an application called Task Management, dedicated entirely to achieving the above

Mission

Our mission is to contribute to the management and organization of projects and tasks of school and university students and leaders and members of teleworking teams to facilitate the work development process and, consequently, increase its efficiency. We provide tools, resources and work environments for the management of projects and tasks, with the purpose of enhancing the productivity of our users. As for teleworking teams, our application also helps to encourage and strengthen collaboration and communication between team members to ensure a good workflow. We are committed to ensuring a successful organization of every project tackled using our application.

Vision

Our vision is to stand out as the leading platform that revolutionizes efficient project and task management in the market. At Task Management, our purpose is to guarantee the efficiency of the organization. Therefore, we train teams to achieve exceptional collaboration, boosting their productivity and improving their workflow. We seek to obtain recognition as the provider of innovative and reliable solutions for school and university students and leaders and members of teleworking teams throughout Lima
